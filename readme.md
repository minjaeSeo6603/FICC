## Case Study Prompt Summary
- 5-year unsecured corporate bond with a 6% annual coupon
- Treasury yield curve is flat at 4% for 5 years
- Conditional default probability is 5% each year
- Assume recovery rate 0, and then recovery of 70 at default
- Questions: bond price, spread, and changes under recovery assumption
